Correct roof insulation saves up to 40% heating costs!

Selbst auf Mallorca fällt manchmal Schnee, investieren sie daher lieber gute Isolierung als in Heizkosten

Today, efficient roof insulation is formally the basic equipment of a new building – In the case of old buildings, subsequent modernization is the order of the day. Comprehensive knowledge of the structure of the roof insulation is essential.

Which construction requires roof insulation with regard to construction and materials, decides according to the circumstances of the individual building and the appropriate insulation metode. A new building has different requirements than an old building.

With correct roof insulation, not only the heating costs of up to 40% can be saved, moisture and drafts are also successfully removed from the living space. Plan now, the various insulation methods, in close coordination with a specialist, evaluate their advantages and disadvantages and leave the construction work to an experienced professional.

With the terraces and balcony waterproofings, it is very important to discharge the water that is occurring effectively and in the long term. For most substructures, reliable sealing is indispensable. Otherwise moisture damage can occur in the form of efflorescence in tiles or natural stone claddings, which can lead to cracks and can cause major damage.

The useful life of wooden floor coverings is also decisively influenced by this, whether moisture can dry unhindered. We therefore work with bituminous welds or liquid plastic, To ensure optimal sealing of your terrace.

In order to be able to get the best out of your outdoor seating even in strong sun or light rain, a good sun protection or a terrace roofing is a perfect complement.
